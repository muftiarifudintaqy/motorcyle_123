What About Motovlog?

The purpose of creating a motovlog is to express yourself, share experiences, and convey messages to the public. Motovlog is a video made while riding a motorbike or other motorized vehicle.

what is a live?

life for motovlog is doing what you like, and that will bring you happiness. You can also apply the same thing in this context, in making a video blog while riding. Without an interest in riding, the content you create will definitely feel bland and less exciting.
